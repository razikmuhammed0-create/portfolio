01 / About

One person. Four crafts.

I'm Razik Muhammed — a filmmaker and marketer from Kerala who builds complete brand systems, not isolated deliverables.

Most creative people specialize in one thing. I deliberately don't. After years of watching clients waste time and money stitching together a videographer, a marketing agency, a brand designer, and an app developer who don't speak the same language — I decided to learn all four crafts properly and offer them as one coherent service.

That means when you work with me, the brand strategy informs the film. The film informs the digital campaign. The campaign informs the app launch. Every piece reinforces every other piece, because one person is holding the whole picture in their head.

I work with founders, small businesses, personal brands, and ambitious individuals across Kerala and India who are tired of fragmented vendor relationships and want someone who will own the outcome end-to-end.

02 / Philosophy

How I actually work.

i.

Strategy before shutter

Beautiful work that doesn't move the business is just decoration. Every project starts with what we're trying to achieve commercially — then I work backwards to the creative.

ii.

One integrated system

Your film, your social content, your website, your app, and your ads should feel like they came from the same brain. They will, because they did.

iii.

Ship and iterate

I'd rather ship something good and improve it than wait six months for perfect. Most brands don't have a perfection problem. They have a momentum problem.
